RAPID Project

RAPID is an ongoing robotics R&D project focused on environmental and industrial protection applications. Further technical details will be made available after completion of the appropriate intellectual property protection steps.

What RAPID does

RAPID aims to detect birds in the operational area, interpret the situation in real time, and trigger deterrence actions.

Robotics Computer Vision AI Solar Infrastructure Climate-positive innovation

Core objectives

  • Develop a functional MVP
  • Integrate bird detection capabilities
  • Test deterrence responses
  • Validate the system through prototype demonstrations

Media & Demonstrations

The images below illustrate the core problem addressed by RAPID, the detection stage, and the deterrence effect when the robotic platform approaches the birds.

Bird activity can significantly dirty solar panels, reducing efficiency and increasing maintenance needs.

AI-based visual detection can identify multiple birds on solar infrastructure in real time.

RAPID is designed to trigger a deterrence response and push birds away from the protected area.
